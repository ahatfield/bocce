Schedule Update: After working through the round-robin format with 7 teams, I've made a slight adjustment to create a more balanced schedule. Scheduling with an odd number of teams isn't easy to make fair, so I've adjusted from what was originally stated:

  • Session 1: Still 12 weeks (Sept 3 - Nov 19)
  • Holiday Break: Starts week of Thanksgiving
  • Session 2: Adjusted to 9 weeks (Jan 7 - Mar 4)
  • Total Season: 21 weeks instead of 24

This 21-week format allows for much better balance in the round-robin scheduling, ensuring each team plays exactly 18 games (3 times against each opponent) with 3 bye weeks fairly distributed. The schedule below reflects these adjustments.
